Discover Selma Raisin Capital of the World

Last Visited:

Selma is widely know for its vineyard as well for rapid growth. The initial growth of Selma was fueled by farming and railroad construction. In 1890, four farmers developed the site along the railroad for a town and began selling the lots. In a short period of three years, Selma was incorporated as a city.
Selma is a quiet and peaceful agricultural community. It has a swimming pool where one can get swimming lessons. The Cultural Arts Center arranges a variety of tutorials, as well as performances.

Selma been located in the center of the valley makes an easy access to travel from the different parts. Traveling from Yosemite National Park to Sequoia National Park, to enjoy the sceneries. As well, to sufur and relax at Pismo beach or take a trip to China town in San Francisco.

Selma provides recreation activities to strengthen community, support economic development, promoting health and wellness, increase cultural unity, protect environmental resources. Provides year-round comprehensive programs for youth, adults, and seniors.

At the start of every year Selma provides a breath taking from the blossom trails, perfect for photoshoots and to just walk around with the whole family. Selma public works maintains over 90 acres of parks and landscaped medians to provide park and recreation areas safe, attractive, and inviting for family gatherings and individual use. As well, for the economic aspect it supports local businesses, providing resources for entrepreneurship and provides top services both big and small.
